How to play:

The goal of the game is to flag every bomb on the board. Left clicking a piece will show how many bombs are around that piece. Right clicking will flag a piece as a bomb. If you left click a bomb you will lose. If you flag a piece that isn't a bomb, you will not be able to flag every bomb resulting in a lose. Left clicking pieces with no surrounding bombs will show all pieces around it and all pieces around its neighboring empty pieces and so on. Good luck!
